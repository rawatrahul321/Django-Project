About Payroll Management System

Payroll is a method of administering employees' salaries in an organization. The process consists of calculation of employee salaries and tax deductions, administrating employee benefits and payment of salaries. Payroll system can also be called as an accounts activity which commences the salary administration of employees in the organization. Our Payroll Management System is fully integrated with accounts and give’s the benefits of simplified Payroll processing and accounting.
